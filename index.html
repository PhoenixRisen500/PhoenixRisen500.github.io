About Me

I've always enjoyed making things.

As a child, I disassembled household items to discover the intricate parts hidden inside. My fascination with complex things turned into hobby electronics and simple web development as a teenager.

After working in information technology at a major charter school management company, I took a detour and learned culinary arts. While cooking elaborate foods in fine dining restaurants, I practiced visualizing my goals, obsessing over details, and managing my time to produce high quality food on time every time.
